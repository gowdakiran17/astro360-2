9. DASHA SANDHI - CRITICAL JUNCTION ANALYSIS
Understanding Dasha Transitions
What is Dasha Sandhi:
Dasha sandhi refers to the junction or transition period between two dashas, particularly between two mahadashas. This is considered a critical and often turbulent time because:

The karma of the previous dasha is completing
The energy of the new dasha is just beginning
Life direction may shift dramatically
Uncertainty and instability common
Results of previous period manifest
Preparation for new phase essential

Duration of Sandhi Period:

Mahadasha Change: Last 6-12 months of outgoing dasha + first 6-12 months of incoming dasha
Antardasha Change: Last 1-2 months of outgoing + first 1-2 months of incoming
Pratyantardasha Change: Last few days of outgoing + first few days of incoming
