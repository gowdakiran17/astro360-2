# Astrological Features List

This document outlines the comprehensive list of features identified for the Astro360 application.

## 1. Dashas & Timelines
- **Multiple Levels of Mahadasha**: Detailed analysis of planetary periods (Mahadasha, Antardasha, Pratyantardasha, etc.).
- **Vimshottari Dasha Remedies**: Specific remedies based on the current dasha.
- **Custom Vimshottari Dasha**: Customizable settings for dasha calculations.
- **Dasha Samdhi - Antar Dasha**: Analysis of the junction periods between dashas.
- **Mudda Dasha**: Specific dasha system used in Varshaphal (Annual Horoscopy).
- **Patyayini Dasha**: A dasha system often used for annual charts.
- **Chara Dasha**: Jaimini system's sign-based dasha.
- **Additional Dasha**: Other dasha systems supported.
- **Cosmic Timeline**: Visual representation of life events and planetary influences over time (Aggregated timeline view of Panchapakshi, Hora, Panchak).
- **Transits & Dashas (Nakshatra Details)**: Combined view for timing events.

## 2. Panchang & Calendar
- **Advanced Panchang**: Comprehensive daily astrological almanac (Tithi, Vara, Nakshatra, Yoga, Karana).
- **Advanced Birth Panchang**: Deep dive into the panchang elements at birth.
- **Muhurta Finder**: Tool for finding auspicious timings.
- **Moorti Nirnaya**: Analysis of the Moon's residence at the time of planetary ingress.
- **Hora Explorer**: Detailed view of planetary hours.
- **Vela Detail**: Analysis of time periods.
- **Panchaka Rahita**: Selection of auspicious timings avoiding the "five errors".
- **Gochara Calendar**: Planetary transit calendar.
- **Moon Phase Calendar**: Tracking lunar phases.
- **Digital Astrology Calendar**: Calendar integration with astrological events.
- **Tithi Yoga**: Analysis of combinations formed by Tithi and other factors.
- **Gandanta dates**: Identification of critical junction points for all planets.
- **Tithi Pravesh Chart**: Annual chart based on the return of the Sun and Moon to their original Tithi.
- **Tithi Devatas**: Deities associated with each Tithi.
- **Detailed Tithi Report**: In-depth analysis of the current Tithi.
- **Amsha and Tithi Table**: Cross-reference table.
- **Watch Hora Alerts** (iOS only): Notifications for planetary hours.
- **Today at a glance**: Quick summary of the day's astrological quality.
- **Upcoming Vedic Birthdays**: Calculations for Tithi-based birthdays.
- **Explore Vara/Tithi/Nakshatra Yogas**: Research tool for panchang combinations.

## 3. Transits (Gochara)
- **Current Transit & Transit Finder**: Real-time positions and search tool for specific transit conditions.
- **Monthly Transit Summary**: Overview of transits for the month.
- **Sign Ingress Report**: Details on planets changing signs.
- **Upcoming Nakshatra transits**: Forecast of planets moving into new Nakshatras.
- **Yogatara transits**: Transits over specific junction stars.
- **Transit Hitlist**: Interactive controls to navigate planetary transits and sensitive points.
- **Nakshatra Transit Updates**: Alerts when planets change Nakshatras.
- **Divisional Chart transits**: Transits applied to Varga charts.
- **Transit Charts**: Visual charts showing transit positions superimposed on natal charts (Past, Present, Future).
- **Upcoming Pushkara Transit**: Transits into nourishing Pushkara Navamsha/Bhaga.
- **Upcoming Planetary Combustion**: Dates for planetary combustion.
- **Upcoming Vargottama Dates**: Dates when planets will be Vargottama.
- **Exaltation Debilitation Transits**: Timing of peak strength and weakness.

## 4. Planetary Analysis & Strengths
- **Shadbala & Shadbala Table**: The six-fold strength of planets.
- **Bhava Bala & Table**: Strength of the houses.
- **Dig Bala**: Directional strength of planets.
- **Planetary Speeds**: velocity analysis (Atichara, Vakri, etc.).
- **Retrograde Planet Dates**: Periods when planets appear to move backward.
- **Vargottama**: Planets occupying the same sign in Rashi (D1) and Navamsa (D9).
- **Bhava Chalit Chart**: House cusps chart.
- **Sudarshan Chakra**: Combined view of Solar, Lunar, and Ascendant charts.
- **Planetary Conjunctions**: Analysis of planets in close proximity.
- **Planetary Friendships**: Maitri (Friendship) table and analysis.
- **Badhaka Planets**: Planets that can cause obstruction (Badhaka).
- **Aprakash Grahas**: Non-luminous "planets" (Gulika, Mandi, etc.).
- **Upa Grahas**: Secondary planets/satellites.
- **Outer planets**: Uranus, Neptune, Pluto analysis.
- **Aspects Table**: Table of planetary aspects.
- **Avasthas**: Planetary states (ignited, happy, sleepy, etc.).
- **Combustion & Eclipses**: Analysis of eclipses and planets too close to the Sun.
- **Debilitation and Neecha Bhanga**: Analysis of cancellation of debilitation.
- **Pars Fortuna / Fortuna Point**: Mathematical point of prosperity.
- **Sensitive Points**: Analysis of specific sensitive degrees in the chart.
- **Distance From Mrtyu Bhaga**: Proximity to fatal degrees.
- **Astronomical Data**: Raw astronomical positions and data.
- **Ephemeris**: Daily planetary table.
- **Ascendant**: Detailed ascendant analysis.

## 5. Divisional Charts (Vargas)
- **Deities of Divisional Charts**: Presiding deities of Varga chart divisions.
- **Planets in Divisional Charts**: Positions of planets across all Varga charts.
- **Nakshatras of all divisional charts**: Nakshatra placements in divisional charts.
- **Arudha of Divisional Charts**: Arudha Padas calculated for Varga charts.
- **Additional Divisional Charts**: Extended list of Varga charts.
- **Drekkana Analysis**:
    - **Drekkanas**: General Drekkana (D3).
    - **Jagannath Drekkana**: Specific variation used for karma/past lives.
    - **Somnath Drekkana**: Another specific variation.
    - **Privitriya Drekkana**: Specific Drekkana type.
- **Solar Return Chart (Varshphal)**: Annual solar return analysis.
- **Amshas**: Analysis of specific dictionary portions.
- **Super Impose Charts**: Overlaying Rasi Tulya Amsha and Amsha Tulya Rashi.

## 6. Jaimini & Special Points
- **Jaimini Karakas**: The 7 or 8 distinct significators in Jaimini astrology.
- **Argala**: Planetary intervention or blocking.
- **Atmakaraka (Soul Planet)**: The planet with the highest degree, signifying the self.
- **Karakas**: General significators (Sthira Karakas etc.).
- **Arudha Padas**: Calculated signs manifesting the houses.
- **Graha Arudhas**: Arudhas of the planets themselves.
- **Surya and Chandra Arudhas**: Specific Arudhas for Sun and Moon.
- **Destiny Point (Bhrigu Bindu) & Alerts**: Sensitive point logic and notifications.
- **Varnada Details**: Analysis of Varnada Lagna and priorities.
- **Filter Profiles by Jaimini Karaka**: Feature to search profiles based on Jaimini indicators.

## 7. Ashtakavarga
- **Advanced Ashtakavarga**: Complete module with Bhinnashtakavarga, Prastaraka, Sarvashtakavarga, and interpretation based on Ascendant, Moon, and Mahadasha lord.
- **Transit Scores**: Compute transit auspiciousness based on Ashtakavarga.

## 8. Nadi, Nakshatra & Chandra
- **Nakshatra Analysis**: Deep dive into various nakshatra attributes based on planets.
- **Research on Nakshatras**: Tools for researching Nakshatra correlations.
- **Unequal Nakshatras**: Support for unequal nakshatra spans.
- **Nakshatra Explorer**: Tool to explore Nakshatra meanings and padas.
- **Nakshatra Pravesha**: Entry of Moon/Planets into Nakshatras.
- **Nakshatra Aspects and Latta**: Specific Nakshatra-based aspects (kicks).
- **Tarabala and Chandrabala**: Strength of the Nakshatra (Tara) and Moon (Chandra).
- **Detailed Tarabala Table (Dinam)**: Daily strength analysis for all Grahas.
- **Panchapakshi**: Biorhythm analysis based on the five birds system (find your Pakshi and align activities).
- **Chandra Kriya, Avsastha, Vela**: Detailed lunar state analysis.
- **Rahu Ketu Analysis**: Karmic axis analysis.

## 9. Remedies, Karma & Spirituality
- **Doshas & Remedies**: Identification of flaws (Doshas) and their cures.
- **Remedies for Specific Transits**:
    - Transit Rahu in Taurus Remedies.
    - Transit Ketu in Scorpio Remedies.
    - Transit Saturn in Capricorn Remedies.
- **Rituals**:
    - Solar Eclipse Rituals (e.g., in Taurus/Mrigashira).
    - Vedic Rituals and Remedies.
- **Personalized Mantras Remedies**: Custom mantras based on the chart.
- **Personalized Nakshatra Remedies**: Remedies based on the birth star.
- **Mantra Remedies**: General mantra prescriptions.
- **Karma Stored in Chakras**: Esoteric analysis of karma in the subtle body.
- **Nara Chakra**: Body part association with Nakshatras.
- **Spirituality**: Indicators of spiritual path and progress.
- **Kalachakra**: Wheel of Time analysis.
- **Bhuta Detail**: Analysis of the five elements (Pancha Mahabhutas).

## 10. KP Astrology
- **KP Astrology**: Krishnamurti Paddhati system features (Sub-lords, ruling planets, etc.).
