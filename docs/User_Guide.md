# Market Timing Intelligence - User Guide

## Introduction
Welcome to the Market Timing Intelligence dashboard. This tool empowers you to make informed trading decisions by combining traditional financial metrics with planetary transit analysis.

## Key Features

### 1. Executive Summary & Live Metrics
- **Market Mood**: Instantly gauge the overall market sentiment (Bullish, Bearish, or Neutral).
- **Volatility Index**: A gauge chart displaying current market volatility. 
  - **Green/Blue**: Low to Normal volatility.
  - **Red**: High volatility (Caution advised).
- **Executive Summary**: A concise AI-generated text explaining the current market outlook based on planetary alignments.

### 2. Market Sentiment Chart
- Visualize sentiment trends over different timeframes.
- **Controls**: Use the toggle buttons (Daily, Weekly, Monthly) to switch views.
- **Interaction**: Hover over the chart to see precise values for specific time points.

### 3. Strategy Performance
- Transparency is key. View the historical accuracy of our astrological model.
- **Win Rate**: The percentage of successful predictions.
- **AI Confidence**: A confidence bar indicating how strong the current signal matches historical winning patterns.

### 4. Active Market Signals (Insights)
- Cards displaying specific risks or opportunities.
- **Risk (Red)**: Potential market downturns or volatility spikes.
- **Opportunity (Green)**: Potential growth areas or buy signals.
- **Intensity**: Indicated by the progress bar (Low, Medium, High).

### 5. Planetary Transit Data
- A detailed table showing the current positions of planets.
- **Retrograde Indicator**: Planets in retrograde motion are highlighted (often associated with market reversals).
- **Zodiac Sign & Nakshatra**: Astrological details for advanced analysis.

## How to Use

1. **Review the Summary**: Start by reading the Executive Summary and checking the Market Mood.
2. **Analyze Signals**: Look at the "Active Market Signals" section. High-intensity signals warrant closer attention.
3. **Check Transits**: If you are an advanced user, review the Planetary Transit Data to understand the astrological basis of the signals.
4. **Export Data**: Click the "Download" icon (next to the timeframe toggles) to save a JSON report of the current market state for your records.

## Troubleshooting
- **"Analysis Unavailable"**: If you see this error, check your internet connection. The dashboard will automatically try to reconnect.
- **Data not updating**: The dashboard updates automatically every 30 seconds. If it seems stuck, try refreshing the page.
